GOOGLE FONTS

Google Fonts is a free, open-source service provided by Google that offers a wide variety of web fonts for use in web design and development. These fonts are optimized for the web and can be easily integrated into websites, making them visually appealing and accessible across different devices and browsers. Google Fonts provides a vast collection of typefaces, including sans-serif, serif, display, handwriting, and more, which can be used to enhance the typography of websites and digital content. Web developers can access Google Fonts by including a simple line of code in their web projects, and the fonts can be customized in terms of size, style, and weight. This service is popular among web designers and developers for its ease of use and its contribution to improving the aesthetics and legibility of web content.
